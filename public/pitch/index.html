The Problem

Crypto payroll forces a terrible trade-off: privacy vs. compliance.

  • 👁️ Salary transparency — On-chain payments expose compensation to everyone
  • 📋 Audit blackouts — Privacy tools block regulators entirely, inviting legal risk
  • 🔗 Address linkability — Reused addresses reveal employer-employee relationships

67% of DAOs cite salary privacy as their #1 blocker for on-chain payroll.

The Solution

Private by default. Auditable by design. Three integrated modules.

🫥

Stealth Payroll

Batch stealth transfers via Umbra. Each employee gets a unique, unlinkable address per pay cycle.

🔑

Scoped Viewing Keys

Grant auditors read-only decryption access. They verify — but can never spend or modify.

📄

PDF Audit Reports

One-click export of decrypted payroll logs. Court-admissible compliance documentation.
