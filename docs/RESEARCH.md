## Mission
Build with Umbra's privacy primitives on Solana — confidential transfers, private swaps, and scoped viewing keys that enable both privacy AND compliance.

## Track Scope & Focus Areas
- **Confidential Transfers** — shielded token sends with zero-knowledge proofs
- **Private Swaps** — DEX trades without revealing order intent
- **Viewing Keys** — scoped read access for auditors and regulators
- **Compliance-Ready Privacy** — privacy that satisfies regulatory requirements
- **Privacy DeFi** — lending, yield, governance with encrypted state
